The day

Circa 1876

The venue is located off of Broke Road, Pokolbin which is quite apt really, we are definitely going to be broke after paying for you mob to drink beer and eat food all day.

See location

What time to be there

The ceremony starts at 3.30pm, that's New South Wales time for all you Queenslanders please don't be an hour late or Koo will be very upset!

After the ceremony is done and dusted the wedding party will go off and have photographs taken for an hour or so, during that time you will be eating canapes and drinking.

It's a free bar where beer and wine will be provided up until we get booted out of the venue. Last orders are at 11pm with closing time scheduled for 11.30pm, for any folk who want the night to continue the groomsman have a house on Mcdonalds Road which will be hosting the afterparty.

What to wear

Smart Casual is our chosen attire.

What does smart casual mean? No jeans, no flip flops (thongs) but don't worry too much you aren't going for an interview!

Getting there

Flying

If you are flying in from the greatest country on the planet (England) then:

  1. you are a very lucky soul, and;
  2. you should take note of the following:

Pokolbin is located in New South Wales which is 2 hours north of Sydney by car, the easiest/cheapest option would be to fly into Sydney and fly out of Sydney then you won't have to worry about organising connecting flights from Brisbane.

Accommodation

Circa 1876 (the venue) is not far from the centre of Pokolbin, therfore accommodation availability shouldn't be a problem. If there are enough of you, you can either rent a house through Air BnB or Stayz or alternativley you can book hotel rooms through Expedia.com. Peppers Convent is the closest hotel only 100m away from the venue.

Renting a car

You can either rent a car through Expedia.com from Sydney Airport if you are an international guest or from Newcastle Airport if you are flying in from Brisvegas. (I am not getting paid for product placement I promise!!)
